When will Summer School start in 2023?

  • The "Summer School" in 2023 will be held from July 6 to August 7!

How to get to the "Summer School"?

  • You can leave a request by phone or send a questionnaire by mail!

When will recruitment start?

  • You can send your applications from April 1st to May 15th!

Where is the Summer School located?

  • We are high in the Tatras! Dolina Chochołowska is our base! But why?

    1. World-famous mountain range!
    2. Beautiful place, coniferous forest!
    3. A lot of rooms: dozens of rooms, halls, offices!
    4. Relative proximity to Zakopane (2-3 hours drive)
    5. Wonderful people in the village who are ready to help us develop the project!

Whose project is this? Who is behind you? Who is in charge of you?

  • We are sponsored and financed directly by the Łódź University of Technology!

Where does the money from education go?

  • Purchase of products!
  • Formation of a first aid kit!
  • Payment for electricity, water, etc!
  • Purchase of small inventory (from a fountain pen to a fire extinguisher)
  • Some camp preparation work (for example, anti-mite treatment)
  • Physician fees!
  • Provision of the kitchen (stoves, firewood, pots)
  • Lots and lots more!
  • Renovation of premises (there is a lot of work)
  • Electrical wiring (I want to have a normal number of outlets in each office)
  • Any equipment (projectors, printers, musical instruments, microscopes, telescopes, etc.)
  • Internet quality improvement!
  • Improvement of the state of the base!

What is the minimum age for a participant?

  • Basic rule: we accept people from the age of 14 or who have completed the 8th grade of school!
